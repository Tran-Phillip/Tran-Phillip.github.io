About Me
Welcome to my page!
My name is Phillip Tran, I am currently a 4th year Statistics major at UC Davis. This is a blog dedicated to cataloging my journey through machine learning and data science, and as such will generally have a bunch of unreleated data analysis and the occational project based on whatever I find interesting at the time. I truly find data science fascinating. I equate it a lot to leggos, where it starts off as a bunch of tiny pieces, scattered across a floor, then gets built up into larger machinations until finally a completed item is made!...except not really. In legos, there is always room to make something better, and certain ideas may not have been apparently the first time around. What if, a new lego set has a part that can make your creation stronger? Perhaps one also didn't think of combining these two parts together at the time to create a new mechanism. True data science is much the same, a endless process, rather than a start to end journey. I hope for this blog to help inspire people in their projects and help further their processes in the wonderful world of data science.
